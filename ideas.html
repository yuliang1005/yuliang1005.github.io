The development of this VR game requires a very high level of technical requirements, and the current physical discomfort that the player may experience during the experience, such as dizziness. Because in the virtual reality game, the player usually moves in the first person perspective, it is likely to have a different degree of vertigo reaction, which is related to the human body adaptability and computer configuration, but the main reason is the motion tracking of the virtual reality device itself. problem. When the person is moving the head, the device needs to detect the movement of the head in time and respond to the adjustment of the display angle to give a real sense of presence. Most of the current devices use the motion tracking sensor to achieve this function, but in the process of using, sometimes the picture can not keep up with the head movement, which will bring obvious dizziness and discomfort to the player, so not only the game is required. Open, you need to develop a new VR device and a computer device that can run the game perfectly. And you need to create a super high-quality video screen, giving players a deeper sense of substitution.
