The last assignment was the first teamwork in our group. This process enabled us to have a preliminary understanding of the information and personality of other team members. Since we are all international students from the same country, there is no problem in discussion and communication. In the initial division of labor, we first know what each person is good at and then assign tasks based on that. Moreover, WeChat is a very popular communication software in China recently, so we decided to use it. If group members have any questions, they could ask others in group chat for help. The Google document shares what each person is working on, so that others could view and add comments even modify parts at any time. The above is the basic methods of our last cooperation. This time around there should be no change, but more attention could be paid to the group's project idea. Each of us should have a deep understanding of it and have our own unique ideas. The most important thing is to have a detailed plan of what we can actually do now. This may require more group discussion and the description of this part will not be assigned to individuals but to everyone.
